Program Overview

42 days. Built to push you.

Our 6-Week Fight Fit Transformation is designed to push your limits and deliver real results in a short window. Train consistently, build strength, improve conditioning, and develop real martial arts skills — with the structure and support to make it stick.

Whether your goal is weight loss, getting in shape, or experiencing the intensity of fight training, this program gives you the system to make it happen.

Who This Is For
Track 01

First-Timers

Never touched a heavy bag or stepped on the mats? Perfect. We'll teach you the fundamentals from day one and scale every session to where you are now.

Track 02

Returning to Training

Off the wagon? Get back on. Six structured weeks is enough to rebuild conditioning, reset habits, and remember why you started in the first place.

Track 03

Curious About Fight Training

Always wanted to try it but didn't know where to start? Six weeks lets you experience the core disciplines safely — and find the one that fits you.

Real Outcomes

What 42 days actually changes.

Most participants finish the program stronger, leaner, and more confident — and many become long-term members. The Transformation isn't a quick-fix gimmick. It's six weeks of real coaching, real classes, and real accountability that build a habit, not just a result.

  • Build strength & conditioning that holds beyond the program
  • Develop real martial arts skill — not just cardio
  • Drop weight (if that's your goal) the sustainable way
  • Earn confidence that carries beyond the mats
