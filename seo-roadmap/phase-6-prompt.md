## What's next (post-Phase 6)

After this phase, the gym should have ~172 SEO pages indexed and ranking. Forward strategy:

1. **Monthly content refresh cadence** — pick 5-10 pages per month for a quick refresh (new testimonials, new internal links, new photos)
2. **Quarterly competitor audit** — check if new MMA gyms in LA have launched and rank for terms you target. Adjust content.
3. **Annual matrix expansion** — add new neighborhoods, new personas, new comparison topics as the local market shifts
4. **Track conversion attribution** — make sure GA4 / lead form events correctly attribute leads to the source page so you know which programmatic pages actually drive walk-ins

If you want a more aggressive next phase, options include:
- **Multimedia expansion:** video embeds on top pages, member-story Q&A interviews
- **Translation phase:** Spanish-language variants for top 30 pages (LA has a large Spanish-speaking audience)
- **Community-content phase:** member-submitted technique breakdowns, fight diaries from competing members
