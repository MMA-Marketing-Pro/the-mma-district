Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

MMA District ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, sign up for a membership, or contact us.

What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us — name, email address, phone number, and any details you share when booking a class, signing up for a membership, or sending us a message. We also collect non-personally-identifying information that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request.

How We Use It

We use your information to respond to your inquiries, schedule your classes, manage your membership, send service-related communications, and improve our gym and website. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Class Bookings & Memberships

If you book a class or sign up for a membership, your information may be processed through our scheduling and payment partners (such as Zen Planner, MindBody, or similar systems). These partners maintain their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as a result.

Contact

For privacy questions or to request a copy or deletion of your personal information, contact us at (323) 990-4494 or visit us at 1205 N Main St, Los Angeles, CA 90012.

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
