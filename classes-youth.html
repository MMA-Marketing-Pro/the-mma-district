Youth Program · Ages 8–15

Youth Kickboxing & Jiu-Jitsu.

Confidence, discipline, and focus — built one class at a time. Kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu for ages 8 to 15, taught in a safe, supportive, and challenging environment.

Ages 8–15 Kickboxing Jiu-Jitsu All Levels
Why the Youth Program

Martial arts give kids more than moves — they build confidence, discipline, and focus that carry into school and life. Our youth program blends Kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu into one fun, structured path where every child learns at their own pace. No experience needed, no ego on the mats — just great habits, real skills, and a lot of hard work that feels like play.

What They'll Learn
  1. Kickboxing basics: stance, footwork, punches, and kicks on pads
  2. Jiu-Jitsu fundamentals: positions, escapes, and safe control on the mats
  3. Coordination, balance, and body awareness through age-appropriate drills
  4. Focus and discipline: listening, effort, and respect for training partners
  5. Confidence and anti-bullying skills built through steady progress

Class Flow

Warm-up games → technical instruction → partner drills → cooldown. Small groups and hands-on coaching keep every child engaged, safe, and progressing.

Who It's For

Ages 8 to 15, all experience levels. Brand-new beginners build from the ground up; returning kids sharpen technique and take on new challenges.

Safety First

Controlled, age-appropriate intensity. Coaches emphasize clean technique, respect, and communication — kids learn to train hard and stay safe.

Confidence & Discipline

More than martial arts. Kids build focus, resilience, and self-belief — plus the tools to stand tall and handle themselves with calm confidence.

What to Bring

Comfortable athletic clothes (t-shirt and shorts or leggings) and a water bottle. No gear required to start — we'll guide you on anything you need later.

Two Disciplines · One Program
01 · Kickboxing

Stand-up striking.

Kids build coordination, timing, and cardio through padwork and footwork drills. Punches and kicks are taught with control — the focus is clean technique, effort, and self-control, never contact for its own sake.

02 · Jiu-Jitsu

Ground control.

On the mats, kids learn leverage over strength — how to stay safe, keep position, and escape. Jiu-Jitsu teaches patience and problem-solving, and gives smaller kids real confidence they can rely on.

Get Started

Give your kid a strong start.

The first class is the hardest step — and it's on us. Bring your child in, meet the coaches, and watch them build confidence from day one. No experience or gear required to start.

Your first class is free — no commitment.
