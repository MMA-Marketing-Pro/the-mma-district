Before You Arrive
1

Arrive 10 Min Early

Check in at the front, sign the waiver if you haven't already, take a quick tour, and get a proper warm-up before the bell.

2

What to Bring

Athletic wear, water, a small towel. For striking: gloves and wraps. For grappling: rash guard or t-shirt and shorts without zippers. We can loan gear.

3

Parking

Street parking. Entrance through the parking lot / gate on Elmyra St. We're at 1205 N Main St — Chinatown / Lincoln Heights, just north of DTLA.
